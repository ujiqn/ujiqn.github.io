1. What the App Does

The App is a remote-control-style app that lets a parent lock and unlock a child's device from the parent's device. This feature is provided using iOS system capabilities such as Family Controls (Screen Time).

  • The parent's device switches between "you can play" and "sleeping" with one tap
  • The switch is delivered to the child's device in real time via push notifications
  • The App is designed to be used together with preschool to elementary-school-aged children
