About Apple Family Sharing

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Apple Family Sharing is a standard Apple feature that brings your family's Apple Accounts together into one group.

If you add your child's Apple Account to your family group, you can prevent your child from deleting Mimarimo or unlinking it on their own.

To use this, your child's Apple Account must have an age of 17 or under. Note that this protection is verified to work on iOS 26 and later; it may not work on older OS versions.

How to Set It Up

The rough steps are:

  1. Open "Settings" on the parent's device
  2. Tap [your name] at the top, then "Family Sharing"
  3. Tap "Add Member" and invite your child's Apple Account. If your child doesn't have one yet, you can create it here

For detailed steps, including how to create a child's Apple Account, see Apple's support pages.
